Executive summary
Two channels, one funnel — Meta scales the audience, Google harvests the intent.

Over the last 30 days, DSI invested $4,643 in paid media and produced 49 total conversions at a blended CPA of roughly $95. The two channels played complementary roles in the funnel — top-of-funnel awareness on the social side and high-intent capture on search.

Meta spent $1,518 to reach 33.5K people across 42.5K impressions, producing 40 leads at $37.94 each (CPC $1.65, CPM $35.70). Google spent $3,126 on 6.3K impressions and 258 clicks to deliver 9 in-platform conversions at $347 CPA — clicks 7.3× pricier than Meta's, but a CTR of 4.12% almost double Meta's 2.17%.
